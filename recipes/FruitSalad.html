Fruit Salad

Healthy Fruit Salad

Fresh fruit is a natural option when considering healthy breakfast foods

Whether you are looking for healthy pancake toppings, incorporating it in a fruit muffin, or a simple garnish for waffles, crepes, scones or even oatmeal, nothing beats the natural goodness of fresh fruits

Ingredients

  • Banana
  • Fruit Salad Dressing
  • Vanilla Extract
  • Apples
  • Mangos
  • Blue Berries

Steps

  1. Add all of the fruit except the banana to a large bowl
  2. Blend together the fruit salad dressing
  3. Pour the dressing over the fruit
  4. Just before serving, stir in the banana. Enjoy!
